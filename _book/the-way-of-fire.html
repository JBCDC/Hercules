The Twelve Labors of Hercules

Chapter 10 The Way of Fire

I Seek The Way

Mantram of Fire

I seek the Way;
I yearn to know.
Visions I see,
and fleeting deep impressions.

Behind the Portal,
on the other side,
lies that which I call home,
for the circle hath been well-nigh trod,
and the end approaches the beginning.

I seek the Way.
All ways my feet have trod.
The Way of Fire
calls me with fierce appeal.
Naught in me seeks the way of peace;
naught in me yearns for earth.

Let the fire rage,
the flames devour;
let all the dross be burnt;
and let me enter through that Gate,
and tread the Way of Fire.

[Added final verse]
I seek the Way;
I yearn to know.
Let me enter through that Gate,
and tread the Way of Fire.

Adapted from the book The Light of the Soul (p. 209) published by the Lucis Trust. Musicalized by José Becerra.

Patanjali’s Yoga Sutras translated by Alice a. Bailey.
